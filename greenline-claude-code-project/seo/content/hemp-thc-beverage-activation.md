Hemp and THC beverages aren't beer. Generic event staff create liability.

Here's the reality: standard sampling agencies send staff who have no idea what Delta-9 THC is, can't explain TAC milligram limits to a curious consumer, and have never been trained in age verification for regulated products. That's a compliance risk your brand can't afford.

Hemp beverage activation requires staff who understand:

- **State-specific hemp regulations** — what's legal to sample, where, and under what conditions
- **Age restriction enforcement** — ID verification protocols that protect the retailer and your brand
- **Responsible sampling standards** — portion sizes, frequency limits, and consumer guidance
- **Product accuracy** — the ability to answer consumer questions about ingredients, potency, and effects without crossing compliance lines
- **Retailer context** — how to operate in a smoke shop vs. a natural grocer vs. a liquor store environment

Greenline's ambassadors have all of this. No other Florida activation agency can say the same.

**Craft Markets & Pop-Up Events**

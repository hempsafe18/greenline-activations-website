## H2: The 50-Point Activation Recap System

Every Greenline activation closes with a 50-point recap delivered within 48 hours.

The recap covers five categories — ten data points each:

1. **Sampling Metrics** — Total samples poured, hourly distribution rate, sample waste, portion compliance, and tasting sequence
2. **Conversion Metrics** — Observed purchase conversions, conversion rate by time block, retailer POS correlation, reorder intent signals
3. **Consumer Conversations** — Most common questions, purchase objections logged, flavor preference data, demographic observations
4. **Retailer Observations** — Staff engagement, shelf positioning feedback, reorder interest, competitive placement notes
5. **Ambassador Performance** — Compliance observations, brand message accuracy, escalation handling, next-activation recommendations

No other activation agency in Florida delivers this level of post-event accountability.
